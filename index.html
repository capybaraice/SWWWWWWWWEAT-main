Production Concept

The inspiration for the establishment of the ドキドキ sweets 100% website comes from a team member who occasionally makes desserts, who will make some small desserts. Nowadays, people see shaped cakes, handmade biscuits, etc., just looking at them, it is pleasing to the eye, and desserts Not just food , It is a commodity that can heal the soul.

Therefore, we focus on exquisite and compact "charm products" that impress consumers' desire for consumption, and can achieve psychological or emotional satisfaction, and pay more attention to the exquisiteness and emotion of products. This is also the origin of the name of our website, which allows consumers to be 100% tempted by desserts from the bottom of their hearts to achieve their desire to buy.
